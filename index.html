Phase 1 Course Goals


1. Foster the creative habits of mind

I feel like I have been able to foster the creative habits of my mind in many different ways in this class. There are little to no guidlines to follow on most assignments which allows me to create almost anything I have in mind. I get to encorporate peices of my life and things I have a passion for, this allows me to be creative and show people a little bit about who I am as a person which is really cool to me.

2. Engage with others in a workshop environment.

I have had great experience with engaging with others in a workshop enviroment. I think it is really important to get used to asking other people for help. Having workshops at the begining of each class helps me build my communication skills and my knowledge of the overal topic we are learning about. I have gotton great amounts of advice from other classmates, they have even helped me to fix or improve my website.

3. Think deeply about craft.

When creating a project, I must think deeply about my craft. I have to make sure that it is appropriate, interesting, appeals to others and myself, and is gonna be something I have a good amount of knowledge on or a good interest in learning more about the topic. I want to make something meaningful as well, so I think about all of the ways I can make my project meaningful to myself and others.

4. Engage in sustained research and skills development.

Within this class I have already learned a lot and built many new useful skills and have more experience in reasearching. We read weekly in this class to get a deeper explanation of what we are learning about. Being able to research and learn more about a topic and then be able to further apply it within my project is very important. I also have picked up skills I either had little to no knowledge about before class this semester. Learing how to code is a skill that is completely new to me and has been super helpful and interesting.

5. Learn to present your work

I have learned how to present my work in this class better than I have before. Being able to show everyone what I have created gives me a sense of pride. This also makes me want to do my best so that when I go to show everyone what I made it can catch their eyes or make them interested in what I have created. On top of that, it makes me take a different approach to some assignments because I have to always make sure that the website would make sense to others and that what I am making is being represented in the correct way.
